## Algorithmic Paradigm:
> - Divide and Conquer algorithm.

## Recurrence relation:
> - T(n) = 2T(n/2) + θ(n)

## Time Complexity:
> - θ(nLogn) in all 3 cases (worst, average and best)

## Auxiliary Space:
> - O(n)

## Stable:
> - Yes
